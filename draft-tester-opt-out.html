Draft Tester Opt-Out

If you want to stop receiving draft versions of SheetDiff, use the instructions below.

How to opt out

Email sameer.mylavarapu21@gmail.com with the subject line SheetDiff Draft Tester Opt-Out.

What to include

Send the request from the same Google account email address you used for draft testing so the opt-out can be processed correctly.

What happens next

Your email address will be removed from the SheetDiff draft tester list. After that, you will no longer receive draft versions through Google Workspace Marketplace draft testing.
